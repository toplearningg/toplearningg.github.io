24. identifikasikan resep nasi goreng dalam bahasa inggris


Fried Rice is Indonesia traditional food. It is very popular food. It is easy to make it. It doesn't take too long time to make. The ingredients are easy to get.How to make Fried Rice? The following is the procedure text how to make fried rice.

Ingredients:

2 cloves of Garlic
2 onions cut into small pieces
1 table spoon of vegetable oil a plate of rice

.First, pound garlic and 2 pinches of salt.

.Second, heat vegetable oil on a frying pan.

.Third, put the pounded garlic and salt, and onions into the hot vegetable oil. Fry them a while.

.Fourth, put one plate of rice. Fry and mix them around 3 menit.

.Fifth, add two pinches of salt. Continue frying until the rice is hot enough.

.Finally, put the fried rice on a plate. Serve with a sunny-side up.

25. Resep nasi goreng dalam bahasa inggris dan artinya


Jawaban:

Material:

- 2 eggs

- 2 tablespoons of cooking oil

- 3 cherry tomatoes (cut to taste)

- 1 plate of rice

- 1 scallion (roughly chopped)

- Crackers (to taste)

- 1 tablespoon margarine

Ground spices:

- 2 cloves of garlic

- 5 pieces of cayenne pepper

- Sweet soy sauce (to taste)

- 3 cloves of shallot

- Salt to taste)

- Sugar (to taste)

How to cook:

a. Heat margarine until melted, cook scrambled eggs and set aside.

b. Add cooking oil and spices, stir well, cook until fragrant.

c. Enter rice into the egg mixture and seasoning, stir well. Add leeks, stir again.

d. Serve with cherry tomato topping and crackers as well as a little celery leaf garnish and red chili slices.

Artinya ;

Bahan:

- 2 telur

- 2 sendok makan minyak goreng

- 3 tomat ceri (potong secukupnya)

- 1 piring nasi

- 1 daun bawang (cincang kasar)

- Kerupuk (sesuai selera)

- 1 sendok makan margarin

Bumbu halus:

- 2 siung bawang putih

- 5 potong cabai rawit

- Kecap manis (secukupnya)

- 3 siung bawang merah

- Garam secukupnya)

- Gula untuk dicicip)

Cara memasak:

Sebuah. Panaskan margarin sampai meleleh, masak telur dadar dan sisihkan.

b. Tambahkan minyak goreng dan rempah-rempah, aduk rata, masak sampai harum.

c. Masukkan nasi ke dalam campuran telur dan bumbu, aduk rata. Tambahkan daun bawang, aduk lagi.

d. Sajikan dengan taburan tomat ceri dan kerupuk serta sedikit hiasan daun seledri dan irisan cabai merah.
